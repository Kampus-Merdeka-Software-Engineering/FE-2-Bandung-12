How To Cancel Reservation?

Should you wish to cancel your reservation or require further assistance, please do not hesitate to reach out to our dedicated Lakeswara Hotel customer service team. You can contact us via email at lakeswarahotel@gmail.com or by calling our reservation hotline at 08123456789. We understand that plans may change, and our team is here to assist you with the cancellation process or address any inquiries you may have. Thank you for choosing Lakeswara Hotel, and we look forward to serving you.

Is the hotel pet-friendly?

Unfortunately, we do not currently accept pets at the hotel. We strive to provide a comfortable environment for all guests, and this policy is in place to ensure an optimal stay experience for everyone. Thank you for your understanding.

What is the check-in and check-out time at the hotel?

Check-in time is at 3:00 PM, and check-out time is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and is subject to room availability. Please contact our front desk in advance to make arrangements.
