Accomodation at Lakeswara

Welcome to our resort complex, an exclusive haven where diversity meets luxury in the realm of accommodation. Our resort complex offers a distinctive array of lodging options, each thoughtfully designed to cater to varying preferences and desires. Nestled within the embrace of our expansive grounds, the resort complex presents a collection of distinct structures, each with its own unique charm and character. Whether you seek the tranquility of lakeside villas, the opulence of modern suites, or the rustic charm of eco-friendly cabins, our resort complex ensures a personalized stay tailored to your individual taste. Indulge in the seamless blend of comfort and elegance as you explore the diverse offerings within our resort complex. From the Heritage Village, where timeless charm meets modern amenities, to the Modern Residences boasting contemporary luxury, every choice promises a distinctive experience.

All Stays Include

  • Round trip transfers from Yogyakarta
  • Customized room amenities
  • Local cultural experiences
  • Culinary tour of Beringharjo Market
  • Photography safari at Indrayanti Beach
  • Mount Merapi Adventure

Tranquil Lotus Lakeside Retreat

Welcome to Tranquil Lotus Lakeside Retreat, an immersive sanctuary that embraces nature's embrace with abundant leaves, plants, and the enchanting beauty of blooming flowers. This unique accommodation is surrounded by a small tranquil lily pond, offering a serene view that complements the lush greenery. Ideal for those seeking a breath of fresh air, the rooms provide a refreshing atmosphere with picturesque scenes of the small lotus-filled lake, creating a harmonious blend of natural charm and tranquil elegance.
